the book spot

the greatest ever book shop in the world

Book spot is an online bookstore with a mission to financially support local, independent bookstores. We believe that bookstores are essential to a healthy culture. They’re where authors can connect with readers, where we discover new writers, where children get hooked on the thrill of reading that can last a lifetime. They’re also anchors for our downtowns and communities.



list of books

Harry Potter
Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows is a rousing culmination of seven books' worth of page-turning plot

It doesn't rely solely on its previous installments to wow you. There are new characters, new dangers, and new twists to keep you guessing. It's a satisfying conclusion to a much-loved series.

A SISTER MORE LIKE ME
A siter more like me is a good book for kids.

Not a retelling of the movie. Beautifully delves into the relationship of the sisters... The story is told in the voices of both Elsa and Anna, and may require a few reads before very young listeners/readers get used to the tandem narrative style. Well worth a buy.

ROBINSON CRUSOE
A good book.

Very nice, interesting classic story. Its comprehension is a little difficult for those who do not read much, for it is a classic edition.The over all story is great. Despite describing the experience of so many years, there is no monotony in the story. However some of the last chapters are somewhat dragging the story which I did not like much.

Rip Van Winkle

Very nice book. The pictures are magnificent. Perfect tribute to Irving. The right one to get to read and show a child that never heard the story before.
